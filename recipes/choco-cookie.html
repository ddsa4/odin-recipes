Crumbs of happyness

  • Butter - 230g
  • White sugar - 200g
  • Brown sugar - 220g
  • Vannila extract - 2 tsp
  • 2 homemade eggs (can be bought from farmers in a local market)
  • Flour - 360g
  • Baking soda - 1 tsp
  • Baking powder - 1/2 tsp
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Chocolate chips - 350g (although I usually put about 500g)

Path to self discovery

  1. First step should be to preheat the oven to precisely 191ÂºC. Also prepare a baking pan with silicon baking mat (because cookies can be environment friendly);
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt in a bowl and leave it on the side;
  3. Cream together butter and sugars until combined;
  4. Add the eggs and vanilla to the cream and beat it (Michael Jackson style) until it gets fluffy;
  5. Mix dry ingredients until combined;
  6. Add the good and sweet chocolate chips and mix well everything;
  7. Take 2 to 3 tbsp of dough, roll it into a ball and then place it in the silicon baking mate. Repeat until you finish the dought (and I mean, really really finish) and place all the balls spaced;
  8. Put it in the preheat over and let it bake for 8 to 10 minutes. Be careful to take them out when they're just becoming to turn brown;
  9. The cookies should sit for 2 minutes in the cooling rack before being completely devoured.
