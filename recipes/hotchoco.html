Crumbs of happyness

  • 250 ml of oat milk or any plant based milk
  • A cinnamon stick
  • 2 tbsp of cocoa powder
  • Vegan whipped cream

Path to self discovery

  1. Pour the milk in a pan and heat it for 5 minutes. To improve, you can sauter the milk before the next step;
  2. Add the chocolate and stir it until the liquid becomes a bit thick;
  3. Finally pour it in a mug, add the whipped cream and cinnamon stick. you can also sprinkle a bit of cocoa powder as well. And voila, it's done!
